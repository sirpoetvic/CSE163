Thriller 25: Limited Japanese Single Collection was a 25th anniversary limited Japanese edition box set released in 2008 for American recording artist Michael Jackson's sixth studio album Thriller. The original album sold between 51 and 65 million copies worldwide, making it the world's best selling album of all time.[2]

Contents[edit]
The limited edition box-set contains a 14-track set of all 7 CD singles that were released, with 2 tracks on each CD plus each disc featuring B-sides. The A-side being the main single taken from the Thriller album. Each CD is housed in mini-LP replica cardboard sleeves with the original artwork including Sony inner sleeves and 'vinyl look' discs, with the set housed in a glossy 5" picture box with Japanese and English lyric booklet & gold 'King Of Pop' obi strip).[3]
