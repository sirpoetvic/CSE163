MJ & Friends were concerts held by American recording artist Michael Jackson in 1999, with numerous other performers as well. The purpose of the tour was to raise funds for children in Kosovo, Africa and elsewhere. Jackson gave two concerts during the tour. The first one took place in Seoul, South Korea on June 25 and the second one was in Munich, Germany. In an interview with Thomas Gottshalk, Jackson was joined by long-time collaborator Slash during the two concerts. She's Out of My Life was reportedly planned for the Seoul concert, but was cancelled
Munich bridge accident[edit]
At the Munich Concert, while performing Earth Song, the middle section of the bridge collapsed after ascending into the air, He was forced to climb out of the pit that the mechanism landed in. Michael, nor the audience was injured. However, Michael was taken to the hospital after the concert. The crash was confirmed to be a mechanical failure from the crane
